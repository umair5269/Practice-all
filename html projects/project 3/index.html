Defination List

Mango
The mango fruit is roughly oval in shape, with uneven sides. The fruit is a drupe, with an outer flesh surrounding a stone. The flesh is soft and bright yellow-orange in color. The skin of the fruit is yellow-green to red.
Apple
The apple is a pome(fleshy) fruit, in which the ripened ovary and surrounding tissue both become fleshy and edible....When harvested, apples are usually roundish,5--10cm (2--4 inches) in diameter, and some shade of red, green, or yellow in colour; they vary in size, shape and acidity depending on the variety.
Grapes
Grapes are fleshy, rounded fruits that grow in clusters made up of many fruits of greenish, yellowish or purple skin. The pulp is juicy and sweet, and it contain several seeds or pips. It is a well-known fruit; it is eaten raw, although it is mainly used for making wine.
